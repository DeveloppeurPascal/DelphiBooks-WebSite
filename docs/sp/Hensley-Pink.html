About Hensley Pink

Hensley Pink is a Jamaica from the district of Lampard, in the Frankfield community of the parish of Clarendon. He is a former classroom teacher. He is an avid writer and self-publisher. He has publications (fictions and non fictions) in eBooks and printed formats, in poetry and prose. He has a passion for learning innovations and has developped computer-based educational products.
