About Tom Swan

Tom Swan is the internationally acclaimed author of over 30 computer books, including IDG Books Worldwide's bestselling Type & Learn(R) C. He is a former contributing editor to PC Techniques and columnist for PC World and Dr. Dobb's Journal.
