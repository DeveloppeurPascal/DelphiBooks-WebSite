About Marco Cantú

Marco is the author of over 20 books on Delphi and the Object Pascal language, including the best-selling Mastering Delphi series and a number of more recent self-published Delphi Handbooks. He also has written books on C++ and other technologies.

Marco lives in Italy and has given Delphi-related and general development sessions at countless conferences in Europe and around the world, including Borland Conferences, the SD West Conference, CodeRage online conferences, Delphi Tage, EKON, and many others.

Marco is one of the RAD Studio Product Managers at Embarcadero, focused on Delphi. For more information, see his web site, www.marcocantu.com.
